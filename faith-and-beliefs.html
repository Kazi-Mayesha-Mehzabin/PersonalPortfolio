Faith and Beliefs

We are unaware of so many things, yet we live our lives pretending to be omniscient. An individual perspective in this world where everyone seems to have a clear idea about you is uncanny. No human should ever kneel to another person's desire. Despite all the odds of life, there lies a feeling of tranquility in being true to yourself. Facing the truth takes courage and not everyone is courageous, but people will not do that. You know why? Because it would then make their life easier.

We are often jeopardized by our past experiences, past decisions and lessons. The lessons we learn from the past isn’t always as explicit as we think it is and it barely changes anything rather it pushes us to the verge of our existence where being alive is just a relief to the other but to us, we are no less than a walking corpse. When we grow, we have these so many dreams brewing up inside of us. We dream of accomplishing the uneven, outrunning the inevitable, but in this period of time, we come across so many realities that it gets abundantly hard to get past the insecurities that we grow. By the time we outweigh the obstacles, these big dreams keep getting smaller and smaller. The only existential agony stays with you is that,you could never understand the world you tried to believe in. What did you believe?

The greatest irony in the history of human kind is people will tell you to accept realities which they think is tolerable, which they think is worthy of believing. They think they are protecting us from human formed apocalypse. Whereas in reality, they are just imposing their incapability’s on us in the name of patronizing. Even nature fights back when things go out of order, where humans are incapacitated and expected to enjoy the inferiority of their circumstances. I would say there is nothing unpleasant in defying the conventions and breaking down the obnoxiousness of a society. We have so many true stories hovering around us though, little do we know about the truth that the beholder holds.

- Kazi Mayesha Mehzabin
